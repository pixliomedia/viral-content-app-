Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Viral Content Manager, we collect only the minimum data required to provide the Service:

  • TikTok Open ID: A unique identifier provided by TikTok during authentication. This is not your username or password.
  • OAuth Access Token: A temporary token used to publish content on your behalf. This token expires and is stored only in your active session.
  • Uploaded Content: Video files you upload are temporarily stored on our servers only for the duration of the upload process to TikTok. Files are deleted immediately after successful upload.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Upload and publish video content to your TikTok profile as directed by you
  • Display your connection status within the application

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or distribute any user data to third parties. Your data is shared only with TikTok's servers as required to perform the content posting functionality you have authorized.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored only in server memory during your active session and are not persisted to any database. Video files are stored temporarily and deleted immediately after upload. We use HTTPS encryption for all communications.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke the application's access to your TikTok account at any time by visiting your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions." Upon revocation, all session data associated with your account is immediately deleted.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data beyond your active session. When you disconnect or your session expires, all data is deleted.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at: your-email@gmail.com
